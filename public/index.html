The Studio

In MRI, k-space holds the raw frequency data before the Fourier transform renders it as an image. That unresolved, generative space — full of signal, not yet structured — is exactly the spirit here.

K-space Studio is where thinking happens before it becomes a framework. Essays, science communication, and structured learning — the intellectual companion to Lummiens, not its copy.
